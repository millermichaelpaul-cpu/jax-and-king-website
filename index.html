Who We Are

Two rhythms.
One standard.

Jax & King is La Jolla’s appointment-only hair salon built for clients who don’t settle. We operate at the intersection of technical precision and editorial vision — where every cut is a structural decision and every color is built to last.

The day is divided by design. The Atelier runs on precision and clarity. The House runs on presence and atmosphere. Not to do more — but to do the right work at the right time.

Located in La Jolla, CA. Serving clients across San Diego who travel for quality over convenience.
