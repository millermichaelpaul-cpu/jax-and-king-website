The Studio

A fresh approach to the salon experience

JAX & KING is an intimate, design-forward salon set inside a charming La Jolla beach bungalow on Herschel Avenue. The space draws on the alchemy of vintage apothecaries and the warmth of a boutique hotel — a place where artistry meets atmosphere.

Founded by Jaylin Seng and Paul Miller, the studio has assembled a diverse team of industry experts who deliver customized color, precision cuts, and editorial-level styling. Every service is built around you — your features, your lifestyle, your vision.

Services
